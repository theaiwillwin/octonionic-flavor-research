# The Octonionic Flavor Bifurcation Theorem

## Critical covariance correction (2026 update)

The squared bifundamental operator

M^{lr}_{ab} = ||[\ell_a, r_b, H]||^2

is NOT a basis-covariant Yukawa operator under independent O(3)_L x O(3)_R rotations due to interference terms. It cannot represent physical flavor observables.

The correct covariant object is the signed bifundamental

Y_{ab} = <[\ell_a, V, H], r_b>

whose singular values are invariant and physically meaningful.
